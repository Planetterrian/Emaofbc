## 1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how **[EMA of BC / your registered legal entity]** ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website and member platform (the "Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Account information:** name, email address, organization, and role.
- **Organization information:** organization name, type, address, website, and membership status.
- **Transaction information:** records of memberships, event registrations, and payments. Card details are processed by Stripe and are not stored on our servers.
- **Usage information:** basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, used to operate and secure the Service.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- create and manage accounts and organization memberships;
- process payments and send transaction confirmations;
- register you for events and award professional development credits;
- send service emails (confirmations, reminders, renewals) according to your notification preferences;
- operate, secure, and improve the Service.

## 4. Legal Basis and Consent

We process personal information to perform our contract with you (membership and event services), to comply with legal obligations, and based on your consent for optional communications. You may withdraw consent for optional emails at any time through your notification preferences.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate the Service, including:

- **Supabase** (database and authentication)
- **Stripe** (payment processing)
- **Resend** (transactional email delivery)
- **Vercel** (application hosting)
- **xAI (Grok)** (AI assistant and content drafting for member-facing Q&A and admin newsletter/event copy tools)

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security commitments. Questions sent to the AI assistant are transmitted to xAI for processing; do not include sensitive personal or payment information in AI chat messages.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account or membership is active and as needed to comply with legal, accounting, and reporting obligations. You may request deletion as described below.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, restricted access to service credentials, and rate limiting on public endpoints. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for professionals and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
