# Frequently Asked Questions

_Last updated: June 22, 2026_

## Membership

**Who can join EMA of BC?**
Organizations in British Columbia involved in emergency management — corporate teams, sole proprietors, and non-governmental organizations — can become members. Individual employees join under their organization's membership.

**How much does membership cost?**
Membership is billed annually by organization tier (corporate, sole proprietor, NGO). Current pricing is shown on the Join page during signup.

**How do I pay?**
Payments are processed securely through Stripe. We accept major credit and debit cards. You will receive an emailed confirmation once payment succeeds.

**When does my membership renew?**
Memberships run for a one-year period. You will receive a renewal reminder by email before your `paid_through` date.

**Can I add team members?**
Yes. Organization admins can invite colleagues from the member portal. Each invited person receives an email to set up their account.

## Events

**How do I register for an event?**
Browse events on the Events page, select one, and complete registration. Free events confirm immediately; paid events require payment via Stripe.

**What is the difference between member and non-member pricing?**
Members receive discounted (or free) pricing on most events. Non-members can attend many events at a standard rate.

**What are PD credits?**
Professional development credits are awarded for attending eligible events. Your credit balance is visible in your portal under PD Credits.

**Can I get a refund?**
Refund eligibility depends on the event. Contact us at the support email below before the event date.

## Account

**I didn't receive my confirmation email. What do I do?**
Check your spam or junk folder first. If it still hasn't arrived, contact support and we will resend it.

**How do I change my notification settings?**
Go to your portal and open Notification Preferences. You can turn individual email types on or off.

**How do I reset my password?**
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. A reset link will be emailed to you.

**How is my data handled?**
See our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md) for details on what we collect and how it is used.
